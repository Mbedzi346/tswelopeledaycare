Day Care Fees

Fees have to be fully paid by the end of November of each year


  • 0-6 Months
  • R 650 / Month
  • 7-12 Months
  • R 550 / Month
  • 1-2 Years
  • R 450 / Month
  • 3-5 Years
  • R 350 / Month

Educational Trips and Uniform

We have three educational trips per year, and all these trips are compulsory for all kids.
Every child should have a school uniform (This helps identify the children)

  • Educational Trips
  • R 420 Total
  • Child Protection Day (R60)
  • Sports Day (R60)
  • Heritage Day (R60)
  • Fun Day (R30)
  • Zoo or Union (R180)
  • Mickey Mouse Hour (R30)
  • T-Shirt
  • R 150 Each
  • Tracksuit
  • R 350 Each
* The uniform is paid for in advance before orders are made
*Figures in the prices table are subject to change without notice
